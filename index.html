About Us

At Master Trim, we specialise in high-quality auto trimming and upholstery services. With years of experience, we’re passionate about transforming vehicle interiors to reflect both style and comfort. Whether you’re looking for custom leather seats, complete interior refurbishments, or simple repairs, we’ve got you covered.

Our mission is to provide exceptional auto trimming services that enhance the comfort, style, and longevity of your vehicle. We believe every vehicle should feel as good as it looks, and we’re committed to delivering nothing less than excellence.

We use only the best materials and the latest techniques to ensure every job is completed to perfection. Whether it’s a classic car, modern vehicle, or anything in between, we treat every project with the utmost care.

Ready to give your vehicle the makeover it deserves? Contact us today for an estimate and let us help bring your vision to life.

Services

Custom Auto Upholstery

Whether you want a completely new look for your vehicle’s interior or need a specific color, texture, or design, our custom auto upholstery service gives you endless options. We specialise in high-quality materials like leather, fabric, and vinyl to create the perfect interior for your car, bakkie, or classic vehicle.

Seat Repair & Restoration

Bring your worn-out seats back to life! Our seat repair and restoration service includes fixing any tears, re-stitching, or reupholstering, all while maintaining the original comfort and style. We work with a wide range of fabrics and leathers to match your needs.

Leather Upholstery

If you're looking for a luxurious and durable option for your car’s interior, our leather upholstery service is the perfect choice. From custom-fit leather seats to leather accents, we can transform your vehicle into something special.

Headliner Installation

A sagging headliner is not just unsightly—it can also damage your car’s interior. Our headliner installation service ensures a clean, tight, and secure fit, bringing your car’s ceiling back to life with quality materials.
